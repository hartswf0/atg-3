Executive Summary: The Ontology of Entanglement

This report serves as a rigorous verification and synthesis of the theoretical groundings for the ATG Trail Ontology. It moves beyond citation checks to excavate the deeper epistemological arguments of David Turnbull ("Gothic" assemblage), Susan Leigh Star (Infrastructure), Charles Goodwin (Professional Vision), and Heinz von Foerster (Second-Order Cybernetics).

The central thesis is that robust agentic systems are not the product of rigid, centralized master planning ("Roman" engineering), but rather the result of "contingent assemblages"‚Äîmessy, ad hoc, and locally optimized collaborations between human intent, machine capability, and environmental constraint ("Gothic" construction).

gothic-assemblage üèõ DEEP
POML FORGE
Building complex systems without a central "master plan" via contingent assemblages, templates, and local standards. The "plan" emerges from interaction, not blueprint.
Primary Source: David Turnbull (2000)

In Masons, Tricksters and Cartographers, Turnbull dismantles the myth that Gothic cathedrals were built by master architects. They were "ad hoc" and "messy," built by disparate groups using "talk, templates, and tradition." [Turnbull 2000]

Feature Roman Mode (Software) Gothic Mode (Agentic AI)
Planning Centralized blueprint Emergent, iterative assemblage
Coordination Universal metrics Boundary objects & templates
Knowledge Unified theory Diffused local knowledges
Deep Leverage: Templates as Boundary Objects

Masons used "templates" (physical cut-outs) to coordinate work without sharing theory. In ATG, Prompt Templates act as these boundary objects‚Äîconstraining the infinite potential of existing LLMs into specific, useful shapes (the "stone" to be cut).

professional-vision üèõ DEEP
POML FORGE
The discursive production of reality through "coding" and "highlighting." Data is not "raw"; it is cooked by the observer's coding scheme.
Primary Source: Charles Goodwin (1994)

Goodwin distinguishes the "Post Hole" (the dirt) from the "Post Mold" (the trace). The expert archaeologist "weaves the post mold into existence" by drawing a line in the dirt. To the student, it is just a stain; to the expert, it is data. [Goodwin 1994]

Deep Leverage: Prompting as Teaching Vision

Prompt Engineering is the transmission of "Professional Vision." The user (expert) uses highlighting (examples, emphasis) to teach the agent (student) to "see" relevant features. "Hallucination" is applying a coding scheme where none belongs‚Äîweaving a post mold from random noise.

second-order-loop üèõ DEEP
CB-BOX ENGINE
The shift from "observed systems" to "observing systems." The observer constructs the system, and is responsible for the reality constructed. "It takes two to language."
Primary Source: Heinz von Foerster (2003)

Objectivity is the delusion that observations can be made without an observer. Meaning is in the interaction, not the signal. [von Foerster 2003]

Edge Scout (2025): Action Guards & Magentic-UI

Magentic-UI operationalizes this by forcing the user to acknowledge their role. Action Guards require human approval for high-stakes actions, ensuring the "human-in-the-loop" accepts the ethical weight of the agent's proposal. [Magentic-UI 2025]

infrastructure ‚úì VERIFIED
