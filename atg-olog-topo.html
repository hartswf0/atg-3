Ontology Structure

Objects (Types)

C
Creator
Person producing digital content
T
Trail
Evolving history of creator's process
A
Artifact
Drafts, versions, clips, exports
M
Metadata
Timestamps, tags, annotations
D
Decision
Edits, choices, branches
S*
Trail-Aware Tool
Tool designed around trail data
P
Pattern
Recurring behavior from trail
I
Intent
Creator's goals, style, vision
