## LEVEL 1: SYNTHESIS (Void Management as Culmination)

**The thesis claim:** Context is material. It has weight, cost, decay rate, and engineering constraints.

**Design principle:** Bound the context. Fixed structure, variable instantiation. The jar organizes the wilderness.

**Historical position:** The void management thesis is where situated cognition meets prompt engineering — the design synthesis of 50 years of context research.

---

## LEVEL 2: CONVERGENCE (1990s-2020s)

### Karpathy's Prompt Engineering (2023)
- "Context engineering is the new prompt engineering"
- Context window as the locus of intelligence
- Tokens as economic units

### Winograd & Flores (1986)
- Computers as tools for conversation
- Breakdown reveals assumptions
- Design for situated action

### Norman's Affordances (1988)
- Design reveals action possibilities
- Context determines perception
- Interface as bounded possibility

**Convergence point:** All three traditions recognize that meaning emerges from constraints, not from unbounded possibility.

---

## LEVEL 3: TRADITIONS (Three Lineages)

### Tradition 1: Situated Cognition
**Key figures:** Suchman, Lave, Hutchins
**Core claim:** Cognition is inseparable from context
**Implication:** Plans are resources, not controllers
**Link to void:** The bounded space IS the plan

### Tradition 2: Thick Description
**Key figures:** Geertz, Bateson
**Core claim:** Meaning requires contextual depth
**Implication:** Thin description strips meaning
**Link to void:** The grid preserves thickness through addressability

### Tradition 3: Systems Thinking
**Key figures:** Meadows, Bateson, Ashby
**Core claim:** Systems are defined by boundaries
**Implication:** Unbounded systems are unmanageable
**Link to void:** 81 cells is the bounded system

---

## LEVEL 4: ORIGINS (1970s-1980s)

### Cybernetics (Wiener, Ashby)
- Feedback loops
- Requisite variety
- Control through constraint

### Ethnomethodology (Garfinkel)
- Meaning is locally produced
- Context is ongoing achievement
- Indexicality of all signs

### Activity Theory (Vygotsky, Leontiev)
- Tools mediate cognition
- Context is historically accumulated
- Zone of proximal development

**Synthesis:** Cognition is tool-mediated, contextual, bounded by structure.

---

## LEVEL 5: PREHISTORY (Before Computers)

### Aristotle — Techne vs Phronesis
- Techne = systematizable skill
- Phronesis = practical wisdom
- Prefigures Scott's techne vs mētis

### Rhetoric (Aristotle, Cicero)
- Context determines appropriate response
- Kairos = the right moment
- Decorum = fitting the situation

### Phenomenology (Husserl, Heidegger)
- Intentionality — consciousness always of something
- Ready-to-hand vs present-at-hand
- Breakdown reveals structure
