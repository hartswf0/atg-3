## LEVEL 1: SOLUTION (The proposed answer)

**Trail-aware infrastructure:** Systems architected from inception to make their own reasoning processes legible.

**Core design shift:** LLM manages void, not scene.

**Practical meaning:**
- AI prepares bounded spaces
- Humans instantiate with judgment
- Both labors visible
- Structure inspectable at any moment

---

## LEVEL 2: PRINCIPLE (The design rule)

**The Legibility Principle:**
A tool that appears to address AI bias is more valuable (institutionally) than one that actually redistributes decision-making power — because the latter requires budget, time, rights, or risk to move.

**The Transfer Principle:**
Tools succeed when they transfer capability, not when they claim responsibility.

**The Visibility Principle:**
Making labor visible breaks mythology. Visible process → distributed accountability.

---

## LEVEL 3: PATHOLOGY (The root cause)

**Haunted tools:** Artifacts where form occludes function, where the signifier of ethics work substitutes for its substance.

**Why haunting happens:**
- Institutional conditions create demand for ethics instruments
- Demand drivers: liability mitigation, ESG compliance, reputational defense
- These drivers reward mythology over transfer
- Procurement over implementation

**The structural paradox:**
The same institutional forces that create demand for ethics tools make those tools ineffective.

---

## LEVEL 4: SYMPTOMS (Observable failures)

| Tool Type | Symptom | Underlying Issue |
|-----------|---------|------------------|
| **Card decks** | Perform deliberation without allocating budget | Form substitutes for resource commitment |
| **Audits** | Capture liability without reducing harm | Documentation substitutes for intervention |
| **Dashboards** | Display metrics without enabling intervention | Visualization substitutes for action |
| **Checklists** | Create compliance evidence without changing practice | Process substitutes for outcome |

**Common pattern:** The signifier replaces the signified. The map becomes the territory (but hollow).
